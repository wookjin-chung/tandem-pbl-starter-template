# Decision Log

## 1. Problem Definition
What is the task?
Why is this task considered for a PoC?

## 2. Assumptions
What assumptions are being made?
Which assumptions are most risky?

## 3. PoC Hypothesis
Under what conditions could this PoC succeed?

## 4. Success Criteria
Define measurable success indicators.
(Time, accuracy, safety, cost, etc.)

## 5. Stop Criteria
At what point should this PoC be stopped?
Why is stopping the correct decision?

## 6. Final Decision
- Proceed
- Modify
- Stop

Explain WHY.
